The FAC has improved its data reliability by pre-populating some information. After a user enters a Unique Entity Identifier (UEI) for a submission, our system automatically retrieves the entity name and address associated with that UEI from SAM.gov. If the entity information is incorrect, users can [update their entity registration in SAM.gov](https://www.fsd.gov/gsafsd_sp/en/entity-registration-how-do-i-update-renew-an-existing-entity-registration?id=kb_article_view&sysparm_article=KB0016307).
